I decided to research the meaning behind dreams and what they can tell us about everyday life. Through research, I have discovered that symbols in dreams are representative of day to day life. Understanding what our dreams mean can help us better understand our lives and what potential stressors could be.

Have you ever woken up from a dream crying? Or super stressed from a dream you had? My project helps people remember their dreams with a dream tracker and then evaluate what common symbols in their dreams could mean..

My name is Jessica Waltzer. I am a senior in the Computer Science Academy and I row. As a student athlete and overall sleep deprieved teenager who dreams frequently, I wanted to create a dream logging app to help people understand their dreams.
